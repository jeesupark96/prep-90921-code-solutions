Indiana Jones

The Last Crusade

In the Last Crusade, Indiana goes on his final crusade. There was a mixed review about the Temple of Doom so Spielberg toned down the dark tone and violence in this movie.

Raiders of the Lost Ark

In this film, Indiana Jones retrieves the Lost Ark from an ancient and important place. It was created in the early 1970s.

Temple of Doom

This film was a product of previous film's success but ultimately it did not live up to the standard of the other films before. It was very intense and imaginative.
